1 Introduction

Avixo ("we," "our," "us," or "the app") is a shared-expense and personal-finance tracking application developed by Nebulixlabz. Avixo lets friends split expenses, simplify debts, and settle up via UPI — while also providing a personal expense tracker with category analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use Avixo, how it is stored and used, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data. We aim for full transparency about our data practices.

Our Core Principles Avixo is a coordination layer for expenses, not a payment service. We never take custody of your money. We collect only the data we need to make the app work. We do not sell your data, do not show ads, and do not run behavioural advertising profiles on you.

2 Information We Collect

To make Avixo work, we collect and store the following categories of information in Google Firebase (Firestore database and Cloud Storage):

Account & Profile Information

  • Google account details — email, display name, and profile photo URL from your Google account when you sign in via Google Sign-In
  • Phone number — entered during onboarding so friends can find you. Unverified. Stored as a profile field, not used for authentication, and not shared with third parties.
  • UPI VPA — your UPI address (e.g. yourname@bank), used only to construct UPI Intent links so others can pay you. We do not validate or use it to move money.
  • Avixo user ID — a unique identifier generated for your account

Group & Expense Data

  • Groups you create or join — group name, emoji, member list, settings, and creation date
  • Expenses — amount, currency, category, who paid, split shares, notes, and timestamps for every expense logged in a group
  • Personal expenses — non-shared expenses you log: amount, category, merchant, notes, and date
  • Receipts — optional receipt photos you choose to upload, stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Settlements — records of UPI Intent dispatches between members: payer, recipient, amount, timestamp, and recipient confirmation status
  • Friendships — the list of users you have added as friends

Device & Technical Data

  • FCM push notification token — an opaque token from Firebase Cloud Messaging used to deliver push notifications to your device
  • Firebase Analytics events — anonymous app usage events (screen views, feature usage) collected by the Firebase Analytics SDK. No personal content (no expense amounts, no chat-like data) is sent to Analytics.
  • Crashlytics reports — automatic crash reports including device model, OS version, app version, and stack traces when the app crashes. These help us fix bugs.
  • App preferences — theme choice, onboarding completion status, and similar settings (stored locally on your device using shared_preferences and flutter_secure_storage)
What we deliberately don't store We do not store your UPI PIN, OTPs, bank account numbers, card details, or payment credentials of any kind. Avixo never sees this information — it stays inside your UPI app.

6 UPI Settlement & Money Custody

We never hold or transfer your money. Avixo is not a Payment Aggregator (PA), Payment Service Provider (PSP), Prepaid Payment Instrument (PPI) issuer, Third-Party Application Provider (TPAP), or any other regulated payment entity. We do not require — and do not hold — any RBI or NPCI licence because we never come into possession of customer funds.

How settlement actually works

  1. You tap "Settle Up" on a debt you owe a friend.
  2. Avixo constructs a standard UPI Intent URL of the form upi://pay?pa=friend@bank&pn=Friend%20Name&am=123.45&tn=Avixo%20settlement&tr=<settlementId> using your friend's stored VPA.
  3. Android hands that URL off to whichever UPI app you choose — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, your bank's UPI app, etc.
  4. You confirm and enter your UPI PIN inside that UPI app. Avixo never sees your PIN.
  5. Funds move bank-to-bank through the NPCI UPI rails. Avixo is not in this path.
  6. Your friend receives a notification in their UPI app and confirms receipt inside Avixo. We record this confirmation as a "settled" event on the original debt.

What this means for your privacy

  • We do not know whether a UPI transaction actually succeeded — we only know that your friend confirmed receipt inside Avixo.
  • We do not have access to your transaction history with your bank or UPI provider.
  • The amount we store is the amount you logged in the app — we do not verify it against any banking record.
  • If your friend never confirms receipt, the debt remains "outstanding" in Avixo. We have no way to resolve this on our own.

7 Data Storage & Security

Where your data lives

  • Google Firebase (operated by Google LLC) — Firestore, Cloud Storage, Authentication, Cloud Functions, FCM, Analytics, and Crashlytics. Data is stored on Google's infrastructure under Google's security controls.
  • Your device — app preferences (theme, onboarding state) stored locally via shared_preferences; sensitive items via flutter_secure_storage (which uses the platform Keystore on Android).

Security measures

  • Encryption in transit: All communication between the app and Firebase uses TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption at rest: Firebase encrypts stored data at rest on Google's infrastructure.
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication via Google Sign-In. Your password (if any) lives with Google, not us.
  • Firestore Security Rules: Server-side rules restrict every read and write so that you can only access your own data and groups you are a member of.
  • App Sandboxing: Android's security model isolates Avixo's local data from other apps on your device.
  • No raw payment credentials: We never receive, store, or transmit UPI PINs, OTPs, card numbers, or bank account numbers.
  • Minimal permissions: Avixo requests only the Android permissions it actually needs (see Section 9).

Limitations of security

No system is 100% secure. While we follow industry best practices and rely on Google's hardened infrastructure, we cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required by applicable law.

UPI Apps & the NPCI UPI Network

When you settle up, Avixo hands a UPI Intent URL to your chosen UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, your bank's UPI app, etc.). From that point forward, the transaction is governed by that UPI app's privacy policy, your bank's terms, and the rules of the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). Avixo is not a party to that transaction.

We do not have access to anything that happens inside your UPI app, and your UPI app does not share transaction confirmations with us.

10 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  1. With other Avixo users you choose to interact with. When you add a friend, create a group, or log a shared expense, the members of that group see the relevant data: your name, profile photo, the expense amount, who paid, the split, your notes, and any receipt you attached. Your phone number is visible to friends who lookup-by-phone added you. Your UPI VPA is visible to anyone you owe money to (since they need it to receive a UPI payment from you, or vice versa).
  2. With our service providers (Firebase / Google). Firebase processes and stores data on our behalf as a processor, under contractual data-protection terms.
  3. With UPI apps you choose to use. When you tap "Settle Up", recipient name, VPA, amount, and a settlement reference are passed to the UPI app you launch.
  4. Where required by law. We may disclose information if compelled by a valid legal process (court order, summons, government request) under applicable Indian or other law. We will challenge overbroad requests where lawful to do so.
  5. To protect rights and safety. If we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to prevent fraud, security incidents, or harm to users.
  6. In a business transfer. If Nebulixlabz is acquired, merged, or sells assets that include Avixo, your data may be transferred — subject to the same protections as this policy.

We categorically do not:

  • Sell your personal data to anyone, for any reason
  • Share data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Provide data to data brokers, credit bureaus, or scoring services
  • Use your data for automated decision-making that affects your legal rights

11 Your Privacy Rights

You have meaningful control over your data:

Access

  • View your profile, groups, expenses, settlements, and receipts directly inside the app at any time.
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you by emailing nebulix.labz@gmail.com.

Correction

  • Edit your display name, phone number, and UPI VPA in your profile settings.
  • Edit or delete individual expenses, groups, and settlements from inside the app.

Deletion

  • Delete individual expenses, receipts, groups, or friendships from inside the app.
  • Delete your entire Avixo account from the in-app Settings → Account → Delete Account flow. This removes your profile, friendships, personal expenses, and the data you contributed.
  • You can also email nebulix.labz@gmail.com to request deletion.
Shared-ledger note Because shared expenses involve multiple people, we cannot fully erase expenses that other group members still rely on for their own balances. When you delete your account, your name is anonymised on records that other members need to keep their ledger consistent. Your phone, VPA, profile photo, and personal expenses are deleted entirely.

Portability

You can request an export of your data in a structured, machine-readable format by emailing us.

Withdraw consent / opt-out

  • Revoke notification permission any time from Android Settings.
  • Sign out at any time from in-app Settings.
  • Disable Firebase Analytics collection from in-app Settings → Privacy (where available).
  • Uninstall the app to stop further data collection from your device.

Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your data, please contact us first — we will work to resolve it. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

12 Retention & Account Deletion

How long we keep data

  • Active accounts: we retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: profile data, personal expenses, receipt photos, and friendships are removed within 30 days of your deletion request. Backup copies are purged within 90 days.
  • Shared expense records: as described above, expenses you logged in a group remain in the ledger of that group (with your identifier anonymised) for as long as other members keep the group, so their balances remain consistent.
  • Crash logs and anonymous analytics: retained for up to 14 months in line with Firebase defaults.
  • Legal obligations: we may retain certain records longer where required by law.

15 Your Responsibilities

To keep your account and data safe:

  • Secure your device. Use a strong screen lock and keep Android updated.
  • Secure your Google account. Turn on 2-Step Verification — your Avixo login depends on it.
  • Verify recipients before paying. Avixo passes the VPA you tap to your UPI app, but final authorisation happens in the UPI app. Always double-check the recipient name shown in your UPI app before entering your PIN.
  • Be honest in shared expenses. Other users rely on what you log.
  • Avoid uploading sensitive content. Don't upload receipts that contain card numbers, government IDs, or other sensitive personal data.
  • Report concerns promptly. If you discover a privacy or security issue, contact us immediately.
